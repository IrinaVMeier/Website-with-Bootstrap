Teaching Philosophy

My primary pedagogical goal is to teach students the strategies of engaging in the process of critical inquiry and to equip them with linguistic and cultural tools for the careers of their choice. Open dialogue, conscientious thinking, power of interconnectedness, and emphasis on the value of individual experience for the well-being of the local and global communities are the tenets of my teaching approach.
